# Terms & Conditions Requirements Document

## Introduction

This document outlines the requirements for creating comprehensive Terms & Conditions for GRM Robotics, a robotics education company. The Terms & Conditions will serve as the legal framework governing the relationship between GRM Robotics and its customers, covering e-commerce transactions, educational services, and digital content.

## Glossary

- **GRM Robotics**: The company providing robotics education kits and services
- **User/Customer**: Any individual or entity using GRM Robotics' website or purchasing products
- **Products**: Physical robotics kits, components, and educational materials
- **Services**: Educational content, tutorials, customer support, and digital resources
- **Website**: The GRM Robotics e-commerce platform and associated digital properties
- **Content**: All educational materials, tutorials, documentation, and intellectual property
- **Order**: A purchase transaction for products or services
- **Account**: User registration and profile on the GRM Robotics platform

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: Legal Framework and Acceptance

**User Story:** As a website visitor, I want to understand the legal terms governing my use of GRM Robotics' services, so that I know my rights and obligations.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a user accesses the website, THE Terms & Conditions SHALL be easily accessible from the footer and checkout process
2. WHEN a user creates an account or makes a purchase, THE system SHALL require explicit acceptance of the Terms & Conditions
3. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL clearly state the effective date and version number
4. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL specify the governing law and jurisdiction (Indian law)
5. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL define the scope of agreement between GRM Robotics and users

### Requirement 2: Product Sales and E-commerce Terms

**User Story:** As a customer, I want clear terms regarding product purchases, pricing, and order fulfillment, so that I understand what I'm agreeing to when buying robotics kits.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL specify product availability and stock limitations
2. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL outline pricing policies including taxes, shipping, and currency
3. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL define order acceptance and confirmation procedures
4. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL specify delivery terms, timeframes, and responsibilities
5. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL address product descriptions, specifications, and accuracy

### Requirement 3: Payment and Billing Terms

**User Story:** As a customer, I want transparent payment terms and billing policies, so that I understand all financial obligations and procedures.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL specify accepted payment methods and processing
2. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL outline billing cycles and payment due dates
3. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL address failed payments and account suspension procedures
4. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL specify refund and chargeback policies
5. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL include currency conversion and international payment terms

### Requirement 4: Returns, Refunds, and Warranty

**User Story:** As a customer, I want clear policies on returns, refunds, and product warranties, so that I know my options if products don't meet expectations.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL specify return eligibility periods and conditions
2. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL outline the return process and customer responsibilities
3. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL define refund processing times and methods
4. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL specify product warranty terms and limitations
5. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL address defective products and replacement procedures

### Requirement 5: Intellectual Property Rights

**User Story:** As a user, I want to understand the intellectual property rights related to educational content and materials, so that I know how I can use the provided resources.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL specify GRM Robotics' ownership of educational content and materials
2. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL define permitted uses of educational content by customers
3. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL address restrictions on copying, distributing, or modifying content
4. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL specify user-generated content ownership and licensing
5. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL include trademark and copyright protection clauses

### Requirement 6: User Accounts and Data Privacy

**User Story:** As a registered user, I want clear terms about my account, personal data handling, and privacy rights, so that I understand how my information is managed.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL specify account creation requirements and responsibilities
2. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL outline data collection, storage, and usage policies
3. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL address account security and password responsibilities
4. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL specify account termination procedures and consequences
5. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL reference the Privacy Policy for detailed data handling information

### Requirement 7: Prohibited Uses and User Conduct

**User Story:** As a platform user, I want to understand acceptable use policies and prohibited activities, so that I can use the service appropriately and safely.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL specify prohibited uses of the website and services
2. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL outline consequences for violating terms of use
3. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL address illegal activities and harmful behavior
4. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL specify content submission guidelines and restrictions
5. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL include enforcement procedures and appeal processes

### Requirement 8: Limitation of Liability and Disclaimers

**User Story:** As a customer, I want to understand the company's liability limitations and disclaimers, so that I know the extent of legal protections and responsibilities.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL specify limitations on GRM Robotics' liability for damages
2. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL include disclaimers for service availability and performance
3. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL address force majeure events and their impact on services
4. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL specify maximum liability amounts and calculation methods
5. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL include educational use disclaimers and safety warnings

### Requirement 9: Termination and Suspension

**User Story:** As a user, I want to understand the conditions under which my account or service access may be terminated, so that I know the consequences of various actions.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL specify grounds for account termination or suspension
2. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL outline the termination process and notification procedures
3. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL address data retention and deletion after termination
4. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL specify effects of termination on pending orders and services
5. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL include procedures for voluntary account closure by users

### Requirement 10: Modifications and Updates

**User Story:** As a user, I want to understand how terms may change over time and how I'll be notified, so that I can stay informed about my legal obligations.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL specify GRM Robotics' right to modify terms
2. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL outline notification procedures for term changes
3. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL specify effective dates for modified terms
4. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL address user options when terms are modified
5. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL include version control and historical term access

### Requirement 11: Educational Services and Content

**User Story:** As an educator or student, I want clear terms regarding educational services and content usage, so that I understand licensing and usage rights for learning materials.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL specify educational content licensing terms
2. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL outline classroom and institutional usage rights
3. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL address content updates and version control
4. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL specify technical support terms for educational services
5. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL include age restrictions and parental consent requirements

### Requirement 12: International Sales and Compliance

**User Story:** As an international customer, I want to understand terms specific to cross-border transactions and local compliance requirements, so that I can purchase products legally and safely.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL specify international shipping terms and restrictions
2. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL address customs, duties, and import responsibilities
3. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL include export control and trade compliance clauses
4. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL specify currency conversion and international payment terms
5. THE Terms & Conditions SHALL address local law compliance and regulatory requirements
